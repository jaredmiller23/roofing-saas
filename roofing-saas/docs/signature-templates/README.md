# Signature Templates for Appalachian Storm Restoration

This directory contains HTML templates for legal documents required by roofing contractors in Tennessee. All templates are designed with placeholders for dynamic data insertion and are compliant with Tennessee state and federal requirements.

### Core Contract Documents
- **`residential-contract.html`** - Complete residential roofing contract with payment terms, scope of work, and warranties
- **`change-order.html`** - Change order form for modifications to original contract scope or pricing
- **`completion-certificate.html`** - Certificate of completion with quality checklist and customer acceptance

### Insurance-Related Documents
- **`aob.html`** - Assignment of Benefits form for insurance claim processing

- **`conditional-lien-release.html`** - Conditional lien waiver and release upon progress payment
- **`unconditional-lien-release.html`** - Unconditional lien waiver and release upon final payment

- **`warranty.html`** - Comprehensive warranty certificate with workmanship and material warranties
- **`ftc-disclosure.html`** - Federal Trade Commission disclosure and consumer rights notice

### Tennessee State Requirements
All templates comply with Tennessee contractor licensing requirements:
- Projects $25,000+ require contractor license (included via `{{license_number}}`)
- 3-day right of rescission for door-to-door sales
- Proper insurance and warranty disclosures
- Compliance with Tennessee mechanics' lien laws

### Federal Requirements
Templates include required federal disclosures:
- **FTC Truth in Advertising** compliance
- **Cooling-Off Rule** (16 CFR 429) for door-to-door sales
- **Consumer protection** disclosures
- **New 2025 fee disclosure rules** (effective May 12, 2025)

### Template Integration

- Placeholders should be populated from database records

- All templates have been designed based on industry standards and legal research
- **IMPORTANT:** All content should be reviewed by a qualified attorney before use
- Templates include standard industry language but may need customization for specific situations
- Local legal counsel should verify compliance with current Tennessee law

### Best Practices
- Always provide customers with copies of signed documents
- Maintain signed originals for legal compliance
- Update templates regularly to reflect law changes
- Use appropriate template for each situation (conditional vs unconditional releases, etc.)
